Partner with Us

Join us in empowering the unemployed by providing essential skill training and support to those in need.

Why Partner with Us?

We believe in the power of collaboration. By partnering with us, you can contribute to skill development initiatives that help shape the future of unemployed individuals, creating a brighter and more skilled workforce.

Educational Institutions

Partner with us to offer specialized skill training courses and workshops to students and adults seeking employment opportunities.

NGOs & Nonprofits

Work alongside us to provide essential training and support to marginalized communities, empowering them with skills to secure stable livelihoods.

Corporates & Businesses

Collaborate with us in offering industry-specific training and internships, providing your business with a skilled workforce while giving back to society.

Government Agencies

Partner with government initiatives to improve the scope and reach of skill training programs that empower the unemployed across the nation.

How to Become a Partner

Becoming a partner with us is easy! Follow the steps below to get started:

  1. Fill out the partnership interest form on our website.
  2. Our team will review your application and get in touch with you to discuss the collaboration.
  3. Sign the partnership agreement, outlining the terms and benefits for both parties.
  4. Launch the partnership and start delivering skill training programs!

Eligibility to Partner

We are looking for organizations that meet the following criteria:

  • Non-profit organizations, educational institutions, government bodies, or corporates with a focus on skill development.
  • A strong track record of community involvement or training programs.
  • Commitment to empowering individuals through education and skill-building.
  • Availability of resources to support the initiative, whether financial, physical, or human resources.
