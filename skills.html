Skill Development & Training

Upgrade your career potential with government-supported and private training programs.

IT & Digital Skills

Get trained in data entry, digital marketing, programming, and computer basics.

Steps to Apply:

  1. Visit the NSDC website.
  2. Select the training program you want to apply for.
  3. Register on the platform with your personal details.
  4. Submit the necessary documents for verification.
  5. Wait for confirmation and further instructions.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Minimum age of 18 years.
  • Basic understanding of computers and internet usage.
  • High school education (10th grade) or equivalent.
  • No prior experience required for most programs.

Handicrafts & Local Art

Learn traditional skills like weaving, pottery, tailoring, and more.

Steps to Apply:

  1. Visit the NIEPID website.
  2. Choose your desired training program.
  3. Fill out the application form with your details.
  4. Submit documents for qualification verification.
  5. Wait for confirmation and details of the training schedule.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Minimum age of 16 years.
  • Basic knowledge of the handicraft or art form is beneficial but not mandatory.
  • Preference for individuals interested in local crafts and sustainable practices.

Construction & Electrician Courses

Skill-based training for high-demand labor sectors with placement assistance.

Steps to Apply:

  1. Visit the MGNREGA website.
  2. Select your region and training program.
  3. Register using your personal and employment details.
  4. Submit any required documents for verification.
  5. Confirm your application and wait for the next steps.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Minimum age of 18 years.
  • Preferably have a basic education (10th grade or above).
  • No prior experience in construction or electrician work required, though prior knowledge is an advantage.

Women Entrepreneurship

Workshops for women to start businesses or become self-employed.

Steps to Apply:

  1. Visit the WEHub Telangana website.
  2. Browse available entrepreneurship programs.
  3. Register on the platform and fill out the application form.
  4. Submit the necessary documents for verification.
  5. Wait for further instructions on your application.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Must be a woman entrepreneur or aspiring entrepreneur.
  • No age limit, but preference is given to women aged 18 and above.
  • Basic educational qualification (10th grade or higher) preferred.
  • Ability to demonstrate interest or prior involvement in business or entrepreneurship.
