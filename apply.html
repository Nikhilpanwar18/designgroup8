Unemployment Benefit Application

Follow the steps below to start your application. This process takes about 10-15 minutes.

  1. Create/Login to your account
  2. Complete your profile with education & job history
  3. Upload required documents (ID, address proof, education proof)
  4. Select the scheme you're eligible for
  5. Submit and track your application
